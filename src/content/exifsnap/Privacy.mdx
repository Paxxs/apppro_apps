# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ExifSnap handles your data and protects your privacy. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users.

## Data Collection

**ExifSnap does not collect any personal data.** The application is designed to function completely offline and does not require internet access to operate. All operations are performed locally on your device.

## Image Data

ExifSnap accesses image files only when you explicitly grant permission through your device's standard photo selection interface. The application:

- Only reads EXIF metadata from images you choose to open
- Only modifies images when you explicitly request to save changes
- Never uploads or transmits your images or their metadata to any server
- Stores no data outside of your device's standard photo library

## Permissions

ExifSnap requires only the following permissions:

- **Photo Library Access**: To read and write images and their metadata

Notably, ExifSnap **does not request or require**:

- Internet access
- Location services
- Background processing
- Contact information
- Any other personal data

## Third-Party Services

ExifSnap does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

## Changes to This Policy

Any future updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the application and on this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support channels provided in the App Store.
