# LastTime Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026  
Effective date: June 20, 2026  
Policy URL: https://apps.apppro.dev/lasttime/privacy

This Privacy Policy explains how LastTime, also displayed as 上次, handles information. LastTime is a local-first personal life maintenance app that helps you record when recurring activities were last completed.

By using LastTime, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Summary

LastTime is designed to keep your personal activity records under your control.

- LastTime stores your maintenance items, completion history, preferences, and related app data locally on your device by default.
- LastTime does not create an account for you and does not require you to sign in.
- LastTime does not operate a custom backend for storing your personal activity content.
- LastTime does not sell your personal information.
- LastTime does not use third-party advertising, advertising identifiers, or behavioral tracking.
- LastTime does not use third-party analytics SDKs for the current version.

Limited network connections may occur for App Store purchases, purchase restoration, and purchase verification as described below.

## 2. Information Stored on Your Device

When you use LastTime, you may create or edit information such as:

- Maintenance item names
- Completion dates and completion history
- Item appearance, ordering, and app preferences
- Pro unlock state and related local entitlement status

This information is stored locally on your device using the app's local storage. We do not receive, upload, sell, rent, or share this personal activity content.

You can delete your local app data by deleting records inside the app where available, or by removing the app from your device. Your operating system may retain backups according to your device, Apple ID, iCloud, or backup settings.

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

LastTime does not collect or transmit:

- Your name, email address, phone number, or contact list
- Location data
- Photo library contents
- Calendar data
- Health data
- Clipboard contents
- Maintenance item names or completion records
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics events describing your app usage

LastTime does not build a profile of you and does not attempt to identify you from your personal activity records.

## 4. Purchases and Pro Unlock

LastTime may offer a permanent Pro unlock through in-app purchase.

Purchases are processed by the Apple App Store. Apple may process information required to complete the purchase, issue receipts, handle refunds, and restore purchases under Apple's own terms and privacy policy.

To verify or restore a Pro unlock, purchase transaction data such as an App Store transaction receipt, token, or signed purchase data may be sent to Apple and to the purchase verification provider used by the app. This verification is used only to confirm whether the Pro unlock is valid. Your maintenance item names, completion records, and other personal activity content are not included in purchase verification.

LastTime does not receive or store your payment card number, billing address, or other full payment credentials.

## 5. Permissions

The current version of LastTime does not require access to location, contacts, camera, photo library, calendar, health data, or microphone permissions to provide its core features.

LastTime may use system capabilities for in-app purchases, local storage, localization, and haptics. These capabilities are used only to provide the feature you request or to keep the app working properly.

## 6. Third-Party Services

LastTime may rely on the following third-party or platform services for limited operational purposes:

- Apple App Store, for in-app purchases, purchase restoration, refunds, and receipt handling
- The purchase verification provider used by the app, for validating Pro unlock status

These services may process technical or transaction information according to their own privacy policies. LastTime does not use third-party advertising networks or third-party analytics SDKs.

## 7. Data Retention

Personal activity content created in LastTime remains on your device until you delete it, reset the app data, or uninstall the app, subject to your operating system and backup settings.

Purchase records and refund history may be retained by Apple as required for App Store operations, tax, fraud prevention, accounting, legal compliance, and customer support.

## 8. Children's Privacy

LastTime is not directed to children for the purpose of collecting personal information. Because the app does not require accounts, advertising identifiers, analytics tracking, or a custom backend for personal activity content, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us through a direct communication channel, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

## 9. Security

LastTime is designed to minimize data exposure by keeping personal activity content on your device. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we recommend keeping your device, operating system, and Apple ID protected with appropriate security settings.

## 10. International Processing

Platform and operational services such as Apple App Store and purchase verification may process information in countries or regions outside your own. Such processing is governed by the relevant service provider's terms and privacy policy.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and publish the revised policy at this URL.

If LastTime introduces new features that collect, sync, or transmit additional personal information, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe those practices before or when the feature becomes available.
